Featured Services

Gentle Professional Cleaning

Professional cleaning includes an examination of your teeth and gums, tooth scaling (removal of plaque) and tooth polishing. It is a procedure to gently loosen and remove tartar that may develop even with careful brushing and flossing, especially in areas that are difficult to reach during routine tooth brushing.

Comfortable Dentistry

our entire dental team is dedicated to making your visit as comfortable and stress-free as possible. Our experts utilize the most up-to-date techniques, tools, and medications to ensure that your experience is positive and painless. You can relax, knowing that you no longer need to let anxiety keep you from enjoying a beautiful, healthy smile.

TMJ / TMD Treatment

Your dentist can determine the cause and proper treatment for temporomandibular disorders (TMD, sometimes referred to as TMJ), which are problems with your jaw and its surrounding muscles. These disorders occur as a result of problems associated with the function of the temporomandibular joint and can result in trouble chewing, a grating or popping noise, or chronic soreness.
